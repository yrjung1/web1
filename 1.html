Notetaking이란 무엇인가?

Whenever I’m listening to anybody, I try to note down the points that most interest or concern me. Afterwards, I can refer back to these notes and act upon them.

Often these notes form the backbone of the types of blog posts you see on my social media channels, or I use them to help tackle a philanthropy challenge, or a business opportunity.

Many of the most effective people I know are avid note-takers – but many people still haven’t caught onto the value of notetaking.
